Hardware

Our test machine is a Linux workstation equipped with two Xeon E5-2690 hexa core CPUs and a NVidia Tesla K20 with 8 GB of RAM. The GPUs are connected via PCI-Express version 3 with a peak bandwidth of 11.2 GB/s.
